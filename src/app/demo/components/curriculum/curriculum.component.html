Curriculum Information
Welcome to the curriculum overview for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (B.Sc. CSE) program. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to provide a comprehensive education that balances theoretical knowledge with practical application. It encompasses core subjects such as algorithms, data structures, computer architecture, and operating systems, along with advanced courses in artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering. This diverse and dynamic curriculum aims to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge required to excel in the ever-evolving field of computer science and engineering, ensuring they are well-prepared to meet the challenges and opportunities of the tech industry.
